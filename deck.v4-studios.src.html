THE PROBLEM

Online gaming has a distribution problem.

The largest operators spend hundreds of millions acquiring traffic — rising affiliate costs, increasing CPA, influencer inflation, weak retention, limited differentiation. The industry competes for the same users through the same channels. Acquisition has become the most expensive part of the business.

OUR THESIS

Win the audience before youmonetize the player.

AIGO is a creator-led gaming company. The monetization engine underneath is a crypto casino — we’re not hiding that. But our edge isn’t another casino: it’s how we acquire. Instead of buying traffic, we build audiences through creator-led entertainment. If that lowers the cost of a player the way we believe, the economics of online gaming change.

THE HYPOTHESIS

Creator communities acquire players cheaper — and keep them.

Audience-first distribution should beat the $250–650 paid CAC and build retention a casino can’t buy on ad platforms.
